# Overall Churn Performance
Total Customers: 6,687
Churned Customers: 1,796
Churn Rate: 26.86%

This indicates that nearly 1 out of every 4 customers left the service, which represents a relatively high churn level and suggests the need for stronger customer retention strategies.

2. Based on the demographic data we found that the most churning was from seniors.

# Key Business Conclusions
Competition is the strongest driver of churn.
Pricing and service quality directly influence customer retention.
Senior customers and medium/high data users are more likely to churn.
Certain states show significantly higher churn risk and should be prioritized for retention strategies.
